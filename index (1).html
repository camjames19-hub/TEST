What We Do

Full-Service
Excavation Solutions

From breaking ground to final grade — JNB handles every phase of your earthwork with precision, the right equipment, and years of Utah-specific experience.

01 🏗️

Foundation Digging

Precision excavation for residential basements, commercial foundations, and structural footings. We dig to spec every time — your build starts on solid ground.

02 ⛏️

Trenching

Clean, accurate trenches for water lines, sewer systems, electrical conduit, and gas lines. We coordinate with utility crews and keep your project moving.

03 🌲

Land Clearing

Tree removal, brush clearing, stump grinding, and full site clearing for new construction, agriculture, and development projects of any size.

04 📐

Grading & Leveling

Precise grading for drainage, road prep, pad preparation, and finish grading. We leave your site exactly how the plans call for — no shortcuts.

05 🔨

Demolition

Safe, efficient demolition of structures, concrete slabs, driveways, and foundations. Debris hauled away and site cleared — ready for what comes next.
